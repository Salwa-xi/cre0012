About the service

We welcome you to visit the Center of Excellence in Renewable Energy and Power Systems at King Abdulaziz University. We offer guided tours to showcase the latest research projects and practical applications in the fields of sustainable energy.
During your visit, you will explore:
• The center’s facilities equipped with cutting-edge technologies.
• Innovative research projects supporting the transition to clean energy.
• Dedicated teams leveraging modern technologies to solve energy challenges.
• Advanced laboratories for analyzing and developing renewable energy systems.

Whether you are a researcher, student, or industrial partner, your visit will provide deep insight into how sustainability is achieved through research and development. Schedule your visit today.
