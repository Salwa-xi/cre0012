Speech by the Director of the Center of Excellence in Renewable Energy and Energy Systems

Director's speech

Dr. Sultan Mohammed Ali Al-Ghamdi

Engineer Sultan Al-Ghamdi's speech about the center

I am pleased to welcome you to the Center of Excellence for Research in Renewable Energy and Power Systems at King Abdulaziz University, which is a fundamental pillar in supporting the achievement of the Kingdom's Vision 2030 in the field of sustainable energy. The center is committed to providing scientific consultations and implementing advanced studies aimed at developing innovative solutions that keep pace with the Kingdom's aspirations for a more sustainable future.

Over the years, the center has gained a distinguished reputation by implementing pioneering research projects in the fields of renewable energy and electricity. The center includes a group of specialized experts and researchers, who work on developing applied research and modern technologies. The administrative team also contributes to enhancing academic and industrial cooperation, achieving influential and sustainable results. The center focuses on advanced research including solar energy, smart grids, energy storage, and electric vehicles, aiming to develop technical solutions that meet the needs of the local and global market. The center also seeks to enhance academic innovation through strategic partnerships and training programs that support human competencies. The center aspires to be a scientific beacon that contributes to sustainable development and raises awareness of clean energy and environmental protection, with a focus on strengthening the links between scientific research and industry to drive innovation and achieve national goals.
