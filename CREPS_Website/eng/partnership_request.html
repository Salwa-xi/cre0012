About the service

At the Center of Excellence in Renewable Energy and Power Systems at King Abdulaziz University, we believe in the importance of building strategic partnerships to enhance innovation in the renewable energy sector.
We collaborate with governmental, academic, and industrial entities to develop technical solutions that support the goals of Saudi Vision 2030. Through partnerships, we aim to:
• Implement joint projects for the development of sustainable energy systems.
• Cooperate in applied research and scientific studies.
• Exchange expertise and knowledge to improve performance in the energy sector.
• Develop innovative technologies that support the transition to clean energy sources.

If you are looking to build a strong partnership that contributes to driving sustainability forward, we look forward to connecting with you and discussing fruitful collaboration opportunities.
