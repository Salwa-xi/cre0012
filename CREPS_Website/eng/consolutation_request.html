About the service

The Center of Excellence in Renewable Energy and Power Systems at King Abdulaziz University offers comprehensive consulting services in the field of renewable energy. With our accumulated expertise and specialized team, we help you achieve sustainability and energy efficiency in your projects. We provide advanced technical and scientific consultations, including:
• Analysis of electrical and renewable energy systems.
• Designing innovative solutions for solar and wind energy.
• Assessing energy efficiency and developing improvement strategies.
• Delivering feasibility studies and technical reports.

Whether you work in the public or private sector, or in academic research, we are here to support you with the best practical and scientific solutions. Contact us now to submit a consultation request and share your needs.
